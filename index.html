About Myself

Growing up I never had a grasp on what I wanted to do with my future. I was raised with my grandparents, and always enjoyed solving their technical needs. There wasn’t much discipline growing up, so I had to work on grasping the understanding of why learning is was so important for my development on my own. I’ve learned the hard way with very little direction. I worked for a hospital for many years, I and didn’t see any growth in myself anymore and had the urge to change my life, do something different but had no idea what that was yet. Resilient Coders opened my eyes to where I wanted to head in the future and I’m thankful for the opportunity. I can now say I have the rest of the tools I need to build the future I want.
